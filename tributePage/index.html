Prithvi Narayan Shah: The Rise and Growth of Nepali Nation
Prithvi Narayan Shah in 1960 after unification of Nepal

Here's a Timeline of Prithivi Narayan Shah's life:

  • 1723 A.D - Born in Gorkha Palace, Gorkha Kingdom
  • 1743 A.D - Prithivi Narayan Shah had ascended to the throne of Gorkha
  • 1743 A.D - His first attempt at invasion of Nuwakot failed and his reign began with an immediate military defeat
  • 1744 A.D - The Nuwakot army was defeated
  • 1757 A.D - His first attempt on Kirtipur was a defeat with a loss of minister kalu pandey
  • 1763 A.D - Prithvi Narayan Shah attacked Kirtipur again after a humiliating defeat and defeated again
  • 1765 A.D - Prithvi Narayan Shah attacked Kirtipur again after two humiliating defeats and finally made the conquest
  • 1763 A.D - the Gorkhas conquered Makwanpur, one of the gateways to Nepal from Bengal
  • 1767 A.D - Prithivi Narayan Shah had conquered the kathmandu Valley (Battle of Kathmandu, Battle of Bhaktapur, and Battle of Lalitpur)
Prithivi Narayan Shah is a hero whose prowess the Nepalese nation owes its existence. By his valiant deeds, Prithvi Narayan transformed Gorkhali nationalism into Nepali nationalism and made it immensely more potent and effective.
